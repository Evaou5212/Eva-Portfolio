Assignment 1 Lost & Found

Description

The language your partner used to describe the image

"It was a cowboy hat that was blackish brown and had a star on it. It had a large brim that went upwards."

Your interpretation of the image

I think it would be quiet similar to the hat that Hudi in toy story wears, but with a larger brim. Besides, I think its color would be darker.

The depiction of the image using code

I used two rotated ellipse and a rectangle to draw the large brim, after that I used a trapezoid and an ellipse to draw the rest part of the hat. To make it look more three-dimensional, I used a lighter color to draw the structure inside the hat. When I finished drawing the body of the hat, I started to draw stars. After I drew all the elements in my partner's description, I found that that there's a lot space in the image, so I started to add a smiling face and a background of desert to match the style of the hat.

The differences in interpretation at all stages of the assignment

After I finish the hat, I want to draw a line between the brim and the body of the hat to make the two parts distinguished. But I found that I can't draw a line in a specific position, so I quit. After I show the drawing to my partner, she said that the color should be more blackish, so later I made the color darker.
